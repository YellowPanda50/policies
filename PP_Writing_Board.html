Privacy Policy for Writing Board

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Short version: Writing Board follows a zero data collection approach by the app itself. We do not create user accounts, do not request profile data, and do not collect personal data directly through our own servers.

The app may display ads through Google AdMob, which may process certain data under Google’s own policies.

1. Who We Are

App Name: Writing Board

Contact Email: yellowpanda50@gmail.com

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Writing Board. It applies to the app and any policy page distributed with it.

3. Data Collected by Writing Board (Zero Collection)

Writing Board does not directly collect, store, transmit, sell, or monetize personal data through its own backend infrastructure.

Specifically, Writing Board does not require:

  • User account registration
  • Name, phone number, address, or identity documents
  • Payment card or banking details
  • Server-side upload of user drawings/content by default

4. Local Device Data and User Actions

Your content (such as drawings) is primarily handled on your device. If you choose to share content using system share actions, that action is initiated by you and then handled by the selected third-party app/service.

5. Advertising and Third-Party Services (AdMob)

Writing Board uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may process data such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device and app information
  • Approximate location signals (where applicable)
  • Ad interaction events

This processing is performed under Google’s own privacy terms and controls.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Ad technologies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Legal Basis and Consent (Where Applicable)

Depending on your region, consent and legal basis requirements may apply to personalized ads or analytics-like ad signals from third-party providers. AdMob/Google tools may present relevant consent flows where required by law.

7. Data Sharing

Because Writing Board does not directly collect personal data, we do not directly share such data with external parties. Third-party SDKs/services (such as AdMob) may process data under their own policies.

8. International Data Transfers

Third-party providers (including Google AdMob) may process information on servers located in various countries. Those transfers are governed by the third party’s legal mechanisms and policies.

9. Children’s Privacy

Writing Board does not knowingly collect personal information from children directly. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by the app, please contact that third party directly and you may also contact us for assistance.

10. Data Security

Writing Board does not maintain user account databases for personal profile storage. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are applied in app development and distribution. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and third-party services operate under their own security controls.

11. Data Retention

Since Writing Board does not directly collect personal data, there is no direct server-side personal data retention by us. Any data processed by third-party providers is subject to their retention policies.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding personal data processed by third parties (for example, access, deletion, objection, portability, withdrawal of consent, or complaint rights). For third-party processing, contact the relevant provider directly (for example, Google).

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app updates, legal requirements, or third-party integration changes. The latest version will be made available with the app and/or related policy files.
