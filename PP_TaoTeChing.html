Such processing is controlled by Google and governed by Google's own policies.

The App may store settings locally on your device (for example, language or display preferences). This local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Because the developer does not directly collect personal data, most data processing relevant to this App is performed by third-party ad providers (AdMob/Google) according to their legal bases and regional requirements.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided data through third-party services, please contact the third-party provider (Google) directly for data rights requests.

6. Your Choices and Controls

While we do not process personal data on our own servers, we aim to keep the App maintained and updated. Third-party SDK security practices are controlled by their respective providers.

Any data processed by third-party services like AdMob may be transferred and processed in countries outside your residence, in accordance with those providers' legal frameworks and safeguards.
