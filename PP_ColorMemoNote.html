Privacy Policy for Color Memo Note

Effective Date: 2026-03-20

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Memo Note handles information when you use the app. The app is designed with a privacy-first approach and is intended to work primarily with local storage on your device.

2. Data Collection by the App (Zero Data Collection)

Color Memo Note does not directly collect personal data from users, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Government ID information
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, photos, or call logs
  • Note content for transmission to our servers

The app does not require account registration and does not operate a user-profile backend.

3. Local Storage and On-Device Processing

Notes, preferences, and optional lock settings are stored locally on your device so that the app can function offline. This local data remains under your device control unless you choose to share content through system share features.

4. Advertising Services (AdMob)

Color Memo Note may display ads served by Google AdMob. While the app itself follows a zero direct data collection model, AdMob and its partners may process certain technical or advertising-related information (for example, device identifiers or ad interaction data) under their own privacy policies and legal obligations.

For details, review: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

The app may request limited permissions only when needed for specific features (for example, notification permission for pinned-note notifications). Permissions are not used to directly collect personal profile data by the app.

6. Data Sharing and Data Sale

We do not sell user personal data. We do not directly share note content with third parties. Any third-party processing related to ads is governed by the third-party provider’s policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Color Memo Note is not specifically directed to children under the age required for independent consent under applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal data through third-party services, please contact those providers directly.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards in app design to support secure local handling of your data. However, no device or software environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Choices

  • You can delete notes at any time within the app.
  • You can clear app data through your device settings.
  • You can manage ad personalization and privacy controls through your Google settings where available.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will include an updated effective date.
