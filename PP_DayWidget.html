Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DayWidget ("we", "our") handles privacy and what information may be processed when you use the app on your mobile device.

App: DayWidget

Developer contact: yellowpanda50@gmail.com

Ad network: Google AdMob

1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the DayWidget mobile application. It does not apply to third-party websites or services linked from the app.

2. Zero Data Collection (What we collect)

We operate a "zero data collection" approach in the sense that we do not collect, store, or transmit personal information from users.

In particular:

  • We do not request an account or login.
  • We do not collect contact lists, SMS, call logs, or similar personal data.
  • We do not run analytics that send data to our servers.
  • We do not have our own backend that receives user data.

3. Local Device Data (On-device only)

The app may store certain user settings locally on your device, such as widget display choices. This local data remains on your device and is not sent to us or to third parties by our servers (because we do not operate servers for user data).

4. Third-Party Advertising: Google AdMob

DayWidget may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Even though we do not collect personal data ourselves, AdMob and Google may process information to show ads and measure ad performance. The types of information Google may use can include:

  • Device and advertising identifiers
  • IP address and approximate location derived from IP
  • Device characteristics and app/ad interaction information
  • Technical information such as browser or app environment (as applicable)

For more details about the technologies and information Google may use for ads, please review Google's ad privacy resources:

  • https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information and we do not share personal information with other companies for our own purposes.

However, third parties such as Google may process information as described in their advertising and privacy policies to deliver ads within the app.

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect or store user data on our servers, we do not retain personal information in our systems.

Any settings stored by the app are retained only on your device and only for as long as the app and device storage maintain them.

7. Your Choices

You may be able to control ad personalization and related settings using Google's Ads settings and your device settings.

  • Ad personalization controls: https://adssettings.google.com/
  • Google Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Availability of these controls can depend on your device and region. This Privacy Policy does not cover how Google or AdMob controls operate, so please consult the links above for the most accurate information.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the app and any local information it stores on users' devices. Because we do not collect personal information on our servers, the primary security focus is on keeping the app functional and preventing unauthorized access to local settings.

9. Children's Privacy

DayWidget is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users (GDPR and similar laws)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or other regions with similar privacy laws, this section explains how those laws may apply.

We do not collect personal information ourselves. Where third parties (including Google AdMob) process information to deliver ads, those third parties process such data in accordance with their own privacy notices.
