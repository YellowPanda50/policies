Privacy Policy for Zhuang Zi

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Zhuang Zi ("the App", "we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the App.

1. Zero Data Collection by the App

We are committed to a zero-data-collection approach at the app level. Zhuang Zi does not directly collect, store, transmit, sell, or share your personal information.

The App does not require account creation and does not ask for personal identifiers such as:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Home or billing address
  • Government ID information

2. Advertising (AdMob)

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. While we do not directly collect personal data, AdMob and its partners may process certain technical or device-related information for ad delivery, performance measurement, fraud prevention, and legal compliance under their own privacy policies.

Depending on your device, settings, and region, ad providers may process data such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Diagnostics and crash data
  • Ad interaction events

Please review Google's policy: Google Privacy Policy

3. Third-Party Services

Third-party services (such as AdMob) are independently responsible for their own data practices. We do not control how those providers process data once it is handled by their systems.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not knowingly directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal data via third-party advertising services in the App, please contact us.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to maintain app security and integrity. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. International Users

If you access the App from outside your home country, third-party providers may process data in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

7. Your Choices

You can limit ad personalization through your device settings and applicable platform privacy controls. You may also choose not to use the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
