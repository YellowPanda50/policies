Privacy Policy for Memo

Last updated: 2026-03-27

Short summary: Memo is designed for zero first-party data collection by the developer. Your notes are stored locally on your device. Memo does not upload your note content to our servers.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Memo ("App"), provided by the developer ("we", "us", or "our").

For privacy questions, contact us at: yellowpanda50@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through our own servers.

Specifically, we do not collect, store, sell, or share the following through our own backend:

  • Your memo content
  • Your name, email, phone number, or address
  • Your account credentials (the App does not require account creation)

3. Local Device Storage

Memo stores your notes locally on your device so the App can function offline and open quickly.

Because data is stored locally, deleting the App or resetting your device may remove your notes unless you back up your device separately.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services (AdMob)

Memo uses Google AdMob to display ads. While we do not directly collect personal data ourselves, AdMob may process device and usage information in accordance with Google's policies for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and measurement.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Depending on your region and device settings, you may be shown consent choices related to personalized ads.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Data processed by third-party SDKs (such as AdMob) is handled under those providers' own privacy terms and policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Memo is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe child personal data has been provided in error, contact us and we will review the issue.

7. Security

Since memo data is stored on your device, you are responsible for device-level protection (for example, screen lock, OS updates, and device backups).

8. International Users

Because third-party services such as AdMob may operate globally, data handled by those services may be processed in countries outside your own.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have privacy rights (such as access, deletion, or objection rights) regarding data handled by third-party services. Requests related to AdMob-processed data should be directed to Google under its policies and tools.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any update will be posted in the App and/or associated policy file with a revised "Last updated" date.
